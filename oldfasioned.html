Old Fashioned

The old fashioned is a favorite of mine. The bourbon will warm you up in the winter and the citrus will be a refreshing treat in the heat of summer. You can enjoy this any time of the year and it only takes 4 ingredients (if you don't count the water).

For this recipe we will need:

  • Bourbon is best but you can really use any whiskey you want. I use Buffalo Trace when available
  • Sugar Cube(s)
  • Orange
  • Aromatic bitters
  • water, both liquid and frozen
  1. place a sugar cube in the bottom of your best tumbler
  2. add 2-3 dashes of bitters right on the cube and let cube dissolve
  3. Add 1 Tbsp water at room temperature
  4. stir until sugar granules are no longer visible, or you get tired of stirring
  5. peel a section of clean orange peel and twist to express the oil
  6. rub the oil on the rim of the glass and carefully place the orange peel on the side of the glass *make it a fancy twist for fun
  7. Place large ice cube, or two smaller cubes, or a small handful of little ice spheres, cubes or chrushed up bits in the glass
  8. Pour 2oz. bourbon over cube and give a stir
  9. While not required you can garnish with a luxardo cherry
  10. Enjoy!
